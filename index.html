How We Grade

We sort every single ball by hand. No robots. Just two very picky 12-year-olds.

Mint

Like new

Looks like it rolled straight out of the sleeve. No scuffs, no marks, full shine. Honestly, the person who lost it probably only hit it once.

Near Mint

Barely played

Might have a tiny scuff or a faded logo if you look really closely. Plays exactly like new — your score won't know the difference.

Good

Plays great

Some visible scuffs or pen marks, but round, solid, and ready for action. Perfect for practice or for holes with a lot of water.

Meet the Trouble

We're 12-year-old twins who love golf — and noticed something: golfers lose a lot of balls. Perfectly good ones! So we started rescuing them.

Every weekend we hunt the ponds, trees, and tall grass at our local courses (with permission!). Then we wash every ball, sort them by brand, and grade them honestly. If we wouldn't play it, we don't sell it.

Why "DoubleTrouble"? Because there are two of us, we find balls twice as fast, and our parents say the name fits.

FAQ

Are used golf balls actually any good?

Yes! Studies (and our own backyard testing) show a clean, undamaged used ball performs basically the same as a new one. The only difference is the price.

Where do the balls come from?

Local courses near us — ponds, woods, and out-of-bounds areas. We always get permission from the course first.

How do I pay?

Right on the website! Add balls to your cart and pay securely by card at checkout. Payments are processed by Stripe, so your card details never touch our site. We ship your order with free shipping anywhere in the continental US.

Can I buy fewer than a dozen?

Right now everything is sold by the dozen — it keeps things simple for us. Email us if you want something special and we'll see what we can do.

What if I'm not happy with my balls?

Tell us! If a ball isn't as good as the grade we gave it, we'll swap it for free. We grade honestly because we want you back.
