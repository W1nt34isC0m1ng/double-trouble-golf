How the rescue works

  1. Hunt. We comb the ponds, woods, and tall grass at local courses (with permission).
  2. Wash. Every ball gets scrubbed until it shines.
  3. Grade. We sort by brand and condition by hand — no robots, just two picky 12-year-olds.
  4. Drop. The best become the weekly Rescue Box, shipped free anywhere in the continental US.
